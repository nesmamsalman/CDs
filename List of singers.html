Faouzia Ouihya (born 5 July 2000), known mononymously as Faouzia, is a Moroccan-Canadian singer-songwriter and musician. She was born in Casablanca, and moved with her family at the age of one to Notre-Dame-de-Lourdes, Manitoba in Canada, before settling in Carman, Manitoba. She speaks Moroccan Darija (an Arabic dialect), English, and French. As of August 14, 2020, her Spotify profile has 1.6 million monthly listeners and 191 thousand followers.
